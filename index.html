Who Are We?

Diverse Gaming was established by three guys who enjoyed playing games. They formed our identity through the diverse games the community has played and continue to play throughout it's growth. We lay the foundation to connecting gamers in an environment that boosts the enjoyment of this hobby. We only associate ourselves with loyal, non-toxic members that promote teamwork, quality fun and clean fun environment.

ACTIVE

You'll rarely see our discord server empty, or not a single DG streamers live. Even if you grow tired of one game, there will always be a friend right next door who will get you hooked onto another game.

GIVEAWAYS & EVENTS

Our streamers hold frequent giveaways and events and they take pride in giving back to the community for the consistent support.

GROWING MEMBERSHIP

One of the greatest things of our community is that there is no requirement to join. If you are a streamer, viewer or fellow gamer, we welcome you to join and grow our brand.

Clean Environment

We don't have any rules, only guidelines. These ensure that we keep our promise of a fun, clean environment. Gaming is a hobby, it allows us to get lost in a virtual world with friends that should only be enjoy constantly.

How To Join?

A simple process. Register your interest and we will get you into our Discord Channel where you can find a team, engage with your favourite streamers, and meet everyone.

Register

Provide your details to us so we can keep you up-to-date with community events, send you a link to our discord channel and introduce you to other members

Application

If you interested in becoming a DG Streamer, provide us with your streaming details and we will get in contact with you to get you on board.

Discord

We will send you the link to our channel, then let the gaming begin!
